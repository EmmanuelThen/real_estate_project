Personal Portfolio

Our personal real estate portfolio is a comprehensive tool designed to help individuals manage and track their real estate investments

  • Property tracking: Users can input information on all their properties, including purchase price, current value, rental income, and any associated expenses. This feature provides an at-a-glance view of the user's entire real estate portfolio.

  • Document storage: Users can upload and store important documents related to each property, such as lease agreements, property inspections, and tax documents.

  • Reporting: The portfolio generates customized reports on the user's portfolio, including a summary of all properties, financial statements, and performance metrics.

  • Goal setting: Users can set investment goals and track progress toward those goals within the portfolio.

Investment Analysis

Our Investment Analysis Tool offers a powerful set of features to help users evaluate the performance of their real estate investments. With just a few inputs, users can calculate important metrics such as:

  • Capitalization Rate

  • Return on Investment

  • Net Operating Income

  • 5-Year Tax Deduction Summary

  • Average Annualized Appreciation

  • ... And so much more!

Property History

Our Property History Tool is an essential feature for real estate investors, homeowners, and buyers looking to gain insight into the history of a property.

  • Ownership History: Provides a comprehensive overview of the property's ownership history, including information on the previous owners, their contact information, and the dates they owned the property

  • Sales History: Provides a detailed sales history of the property, including the dates of previous sales, the sale price, and any other relevant data points.

  • Property Characteristics: Provides detailed information on the property's characteristics, including its size, zoning, and other important data points that can impact its value.

  • Public Records: Access public records related to the property, including tax records, building permits, and other important documents.

  • Neighborhood Data: Provides information about the property's neighborhood, including data on nearby schools, crime rates, and other important data points.
